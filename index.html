How to Play

  • Modes: Play Classic Genre, Artist-Specific, or Guess the Movie!
  • Today Three: A daily synced 3-round challenge spanning the 80s, 90s, and 2000s.
  • The Lifeline (Easy/Medium): You have 20 seconds to type an answer. If you get stuck at 10 seconds, Multiple Choice options will automatically appear to save you. (Or press the button to force it early!)
  • Epic Intros (Hard): Set the level to Hard to only get a 10-second snippet (No lifeline!).
  • 1000 Point Ceiling: Scores are mathematically normalized to a perfect 1000, no matter how many rounds you play.
